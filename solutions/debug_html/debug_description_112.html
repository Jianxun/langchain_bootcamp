Electroencephalogram (EEG) Measurement Solutions

Analog Devices’ electroencephalogram (EEG) application solutions are powered by low-noise signal chain products that deliver results with the highest precision and resolution. Our best-in-class ADCs and amps are critical to achieving accurate and robust EEG measurements, which demands down-to-microvolt levels of biometric measurement. We meet that need with a range of discrete and integrated amplifiers and analog-to-digital converters for both bedside and portable use cases.

Show More
 

Value and Benefits

Our wide range of discrete and integrated analog front ends (AFE), data conversion, and power solutions help our customers design the next generation of state-of-the-art wearable EEG devices. Our high-performance microprocessors, motion control, and consumable authentication RF interface solutions enhance real-time connectivity and allow safety features to be built in.

Our wide range of components covering analog and digital functions help our customers design the next generation of state-of-the-art wearable EEG devices.
