EVSE Systems

Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI) offers a broad portfolio of devices and technology ideal for both AC and DC electric vehicle supply equipment (EVSE) systems. ADI’s solutions enable you to customize the design for cord-set applications at 3.6kW and fixed AC systems for 7kW to 22Kw charging applications.
Our portfolio of EVSE-ready solutions includes:

  • Industry-leading metrology solutions that are agnostic to your current sensor of choice (shunt, CTs, or Rogowski coils)
  • Crypto coprocessors that support digital security and open charge point protocol (OCPP) requirements
  • Precision and analog functions
  • Interfaces and communications
  • Microcontrollers
  • High-voltage power conversion with isolation based on ADI’s proprietary iCoupler® technology.

ADI’s power management portfolio can help you achieve an optimal outcome between system performance and cost in your EVSE, with a wide selection of discrete controllers, regulators, converters and modules to choose from.

Value and Benefits

ADI offers system-level technologies for both hardware and software, all designed to applicable standards including IEC-61851. ADI solutions can help EVSE designers achieve integrated isolation and reduce component count while also meeting applicable safety requirements.

Our systems are fully customizable to your application with open-source software stacks. The application is configured through EVerest, the open-source modular framework for setting up a full stack environment for EV charging.
