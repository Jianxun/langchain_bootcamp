Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) and Distributed Control Systems (DCS)

The transition to Industry 4.0 requires a partner with deep industrial expertise and a strong history of bringing revolutionary technology advances to the industrial market. Analog Devices is already providing programmable logic controller (PLC) and distributed control system (DCS) solutions that enable you to deliver on the products that your customers need today to meet their manufacturing and processing demands.
We’re also preparing for tomorrow. As industrial systems become more distributed, there will be even greater demands on capacity, configurability, and efficiency. Connect, Control, and Interpret

Analog Devices offers signal chain solutions for a range of PLC and DCS modules, including analog input modules, analog output modules, digital IO modules, communication modules, power modules, and products to power and connect Central Processing Unit (CPU) modules. Integrated power management and analog front end (AFE) single-chip solutions allow compact form factors. Ultralow power micro-controller units (MCUs) and Ethernet switch/PHY combinations process the necessary data for communications and IO modules, including software-configurable IO modules and remote IO modules, while secure authentication and co-processors meet global automation trends of integrating enhanced security.
