mmWave Communication Solutions

5G millimeter wave (mmWave) unlocks unprecedented capacity and ultralow latency by leveraging frequencies above 24 GHz for wireless communications. Our portfolio of 5G mmWave solutions breaks the current narrow-band vendor paradigm by optimizing power consumption, bandwidth, and performance while delivering the highest level of integration.

Show More
 

Value and Benefits

mmWave fills an important missing link in 5G networks: increased capacity with ultrafast speeds due to the larger available swaths of bandwidth between 24 GHz to 48.2 GHz. 5G mmWave fits well in dense urban environments where networks will be under strain to handle the increasing need for more capacity and data. 

Businesses in vertical markets will also feel its effects, whether it’s the factory automation of Industry 4.0, lifesaving virtual healthcare and procedures finally becoming available, or bringing reliable high-speed broadband through fixed wireless access (FWA).

With unparalleled system-level expertise, best-in-class technologies across the RF spectrum, and unrivaled, deep domain knowledge across markets, Analog Devices is uniquely positioned to help customers navigate the complexity of the 5G transformation. You can confidently build, test, and harness 5G’s capabilities faster, with higher performance and less risk.
