Vital Sign Data Collection

Our vital signs monitoring portfolio allows continuous measurements to be made. With AI, these measurements will allow actionable insights and decisions, enhancing quality of life. Our solutions include optical sensors, bioelectric analog front ends, bioimpedance engines, low power motion sensors, and high-precision temperature sensors. Our integrated optical modules or sensor interfaces enable photoplethysmography (PPG) measurement for optical heart rate, heart rate variability, and pulse oximetry (SpO2). Our high-precision and low-power analog front ends enable bioelectric measurement for electrocardiogram (ECG), EMG, EEG, and similar applications.
