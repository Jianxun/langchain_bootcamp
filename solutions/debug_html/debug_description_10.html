Electronic Surveillance and Countermeasures

Surveillance receivers utilize our wideband ADCs, RF transceivers, low-noise amplifiers, and mixer technology to achieve unparalleled levels of sensitivity and detection. Our RF DACs and advanced synthesizers enable sophisticated signal generation to support advanced countermeasures. Amplifiers using GaN processes offer higher power and efficiency in smaller footprints and can serve as TWT replacements using our specialized combining techniques.
