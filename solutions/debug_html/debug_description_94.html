Diabetes Monitoring and Diagnostic Solutions

Diabetes technology enables individuals who suffer from diabetes to manage their condition while enjoying full quality of life. Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disorder caused by inadequate production or use of insulin, a hormone produced by the pancreas that allows the body to use and store glucose. Older diabetes technology required individuals to check their blood glucose levels manually through frequent and invasive blood tests. Modern diabetes technology has enabled continuous glucose monitoring to reduce or eliminate the frequency of these disruptive blood tests.
