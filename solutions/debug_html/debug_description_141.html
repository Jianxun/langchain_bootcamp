Motor Drive Solutions

By controlling motor speed, a variable speed drive can significantly improve the motor’s efficiency, leading to energy savings, increased productivity, and extended equipment lifetime. Analog Devices provides signal chain solutions that integrate superior precision feedback, current sensing, isolation, power management, and seamless connectivity to deliver deterministic motion solutions.

Value and Benefits

The evolution from fixed speed to variable speed motor drive systems has enabled higher performance, reduced manufacturing time, improved productivity, and optimized energy consumption across the factory floor. We provide complete signal chain solutions incorporating high-performance current and voltage sensing, robust isolation, high density power management, and seamless connectivity. These deliver advanced motion solutions that increase productivity, extend equipment lifetime, and accelerate lower carbon manufacturing.
