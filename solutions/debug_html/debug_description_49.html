Leading the Intelligent Edge of Vehicle Electrification

We are working with several OEMs, battery manufacturers, and other stakeholders in the EV value chain to build information ecosystems across EV sub-systems.

We are synthesizing the ADI Recharge™ portfolio into three platform areas, where each area corresponds to how we can interact with energy in the ecosystem: energy management, power conversion, and energy storage. We make systems smaller, lighter, and more efficient without compromising performance.

From enabling longer range EVs and better lifetime value of the EV battery to improved efficiency of the electric powertrain and decreased charge times, we deliver solutions for battery management, high voltage isolation, battery formation and testing, position speed sensing, and current sensing.
