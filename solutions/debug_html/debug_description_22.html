Missiles and Precision Munitions Solutions

Analog Devices is committed to supporting the warfighter. We join with defense industry partners to create modern, high-performance, integrated electronics solutions for the newest generation of missiles and munitions designs, along with upgrades for existing platforms.

Show More
 

Value and Benefits

Our catalog of components for missile and precision munition subsystems supports targeting systems, guidance control, fuzing, ordnance stabilization, and advanced communications.

From minimizing SWaP to surviving extreme environments, we provide solutions with the industry’s broadest component portfolio that can help you better achieve your performance and reliability goals.

Low noise targeting and seeker head solutions that minimize SWaP

SDR Transceivers provide military telemetry with heightened performance, flexibility, and security capabilities

Delivers cost-effective inertial MEMS performance needed for modern munition design
