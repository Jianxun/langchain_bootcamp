Energy Storage Solutions

Energy storage is an essential enabler of renewable energy generation, providing a stable supply of energy when needed by supplementing the intermittent characteristics of renewable energy sources. Analog Devices’ system-level expertise in energy storage applications is helping improve grid resiliency and energy efficiency, enabling the clean energy ecosystem to expand and transform.

We offer a broad portfolio solution across the residential, commercial, and utility-scale configurations, which includes state-of-the-art battery management systems (BMS) and high-voltage power conversion with isolation based on our proprietary iCoupler technology. Our power management portfolio, which includes discrete controllers, monolithic converters, and modules, helps you achieve an optimal outcome between system performance and cost.

Enabling a Better Total Cost of Ownership for ESS

We are leveraging our unique position in the energy ecosystem and our system-level design thinking to help accelerate the transition to a clean energy grid. Our solutions for energy storage systems are supportive of multiple battery chemistries and for multi-pack designs. Our leading precision-sensing capabilities ensure quality insights to enable reliable applications for a longer lifetime—helping to increase the lifetime value of the battery. We deliver solutions for both passive and active cell balancing and help maximize the battery’s usable capacity.
