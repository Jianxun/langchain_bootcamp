Precision Current Sensing and Amplifier Solutions

Precision current sensing signal chains offer a wide range of accurate, robust, and industry-proven current measurement solutions that can be adopted in all environments to measure current in low, medium, or high-power applications. Analog Devices’ precision current sensing signal chains have been developed by combining extensive application-level knowledge with best-in-class current sensing technologies, enabling precise and robust measurement of DC and AC. They cover the most common industrial sensors such as shunt, Hall effect, and current transformers to address your specific measurement needs.

Our technology combines precision amplifiers, voltage references, analog-to-digital converters, and isolation products to empower designers to confidently build a solution optimized for their most important parameters.

Show More
 

Value and Benefits

Our precision current sensing signal chains can be applied to solve your most difficult current measurement challenges in applications as diverse as motor control inverters, DC-to-DC converters, valve/solenoid control, protective relays, AC-to-DC metering, and PV inverters.
