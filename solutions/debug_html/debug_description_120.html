Automated Test Equipment

For production IC test applications that demand high-performance, cost-effective solutions, Analog Devices offers leading-edge integrated pin electronics (PE), device power supplies (DPS), and parametric measurement units (PMU). These ATE application-specific products, along with our broad portfolio of standard products ranging from MEMS switches to high-performance converters to RF transceivers, provide unique solutions for all semiconductor test categories, including digital SoC Test, memory and wafer test, RF and mmWave test, high-voltage device test, system level test, and more.
