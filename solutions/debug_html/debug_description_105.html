Therapy Device Solutions for Healthcare

Therapy devices provide support and treatment for both critical care and long-term chronic disease. They must operate in ways that are safe and accurate while providing convenient solutions for both the patient and the hospital staff. Therapy devices range from home care products like CPAP to large clinical therapy devices such as dialysis machines, ventilators, and infusion pumps. Our integrated power management, high-performance signal chain, and protection and consumable authentication solutions allow designs to be highly functional and compact with a longer battery life, and to improve patient safety.
