Gigabit Multimedia Serial Link (GMSL) Technology

Gigabit Multimedia Serial Link (GMSL™) from Analog Devices is a cost-efficient, simple, and scalable SerDes technology. It is designed to achieve a new level of performance for high-speed video links. It provides reliable transport of high-resolution digital video for camera and display-based applications, including autonomy, infotainment, safety, and monitoring. With millions of GMSL links found today on-road from over 25 automotive manufacturers, our solution enables emerging applications across industrial, consumer, healthcare, aerospace, and instrumentation markets.

Extending GMSL’s Proven Capabilities Beyond Automotive

Since 2004, GMSL links have altogether traveled over 20 trillion kilometers, or more than two light years, improving road safety, user interfaces, and cabin entertainment for millions of people. Every mile provides valuable insights to improve the next generation of GMSL and achieve a new standard for functional safety in video links with its leading ASIL compliant solutions. Using these insights, we have extended GMSL’s proven capability into new markets for emerging applications requiring high-bandwidth and low latency digital-video connectivity. It is a cost-efficient, simple, and scalable SerDes technology.
GMSL features robust PHY and extremely low BER. It supports high resolution and low latency camera and display connectivity requirements as well as flexible topologies via video splitting, daisy chaining, and sensor data aggregation. It is a highly scalable and flexible solution, delivering footprint compatible speed options and allows for backwards compatibility to the previous GMSL generation.

GMSL is also a budget conscious solution, as video, audio, control, data, and power can be transported on a single channel. It enables Video splitting as well as daisy-chained displays. Supportive of ASIL B-rated devices and meeting stringent EMI/EMC requirements, GMSL is a functional safe solution with real-time link diagnostic capabilities to ensure reliable and optimized system operations. Its energy efficiency enables lightweight designs and reduced energy consumption and emissions, helping to ensure low power and material wastage across the product lifespan.
